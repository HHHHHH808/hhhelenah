Helenah is a creative company found­ed by Martijn De Bondt (’93). He studied Music Pro­duc­tion at the con­ser­vatory of Ghent.


Martijn star­ted pro­du­cing music at the age of 15 and now cre­ates under the name Helenah. He has delivered audio work for VRT, cre­ated sound­tracks for audio-visual projects and mixes and com­poses for vari­ous projects. He’s also act­ive as per­man­ent live-mix­er for some form­a­tions.


Services:
Sound design
Mu­sic com­pos­i­tion
Dia­logue and sound clean-up
mixing and mas­ter­ing
